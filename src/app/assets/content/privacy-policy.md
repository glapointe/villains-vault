# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 19, 2026

Falchion Consulting, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Villains Vault mobile and web application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

You can use Villains Vault to view race results without creating an account. Personal account information is only collected if you choose to authenticate for future personalization and community features.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information from Social Authentication Providers (Optional)

Authentication is optional. You can view race results without creating an account. If you choose to authenticate via Google, Apple, Facebook, or Microsoft for future personalization and community features, we receive:

- Your name
- Email address
- Profile picture (if available)
- Unique user identifier from the provider

### 1.2 Publicly Available Race Results

We retrieve and display publicly available runDisney race results from Track Shack Racing's timing systems. This data is already public and includes:

- Race times and splits
- Event names and dates
- Performance statistics and rankings
- Participant names as published by Track Shack Racing

You do not provide this data to us directly; we aggregate it from publicly available sources to present it in a convenient format.

### 1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

- Device information (type, operating system, browser)
- IP address and general location
- App usage patterns and interactions
- Session recordings via Microsoft Clarity (for troubleshooting)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the App
- Authenticate and identify you (if you choose to create an account)
- Display your publicly available race results from Track Shack Racing
- Provide analytics and visualizations of your race performance
- Display weather conditions for race events
- Improve user experience and app functionality
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Communicate with you about the App

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

### • Auth0 (Authentication - Optional)

If you choose to create an account, Auth0 manages user authentication and social login. Auth0 only collects information if you authenticate. See Auth0's privacy policy

### • Microsoft Clarity (Analytics)

Provides session recordings and analytics to improve user experience. Clarity tracks user interactions, page views, and usage patterns. See Microsoft's privacy policy at https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

## 4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Your data is stored on secure servers and encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Race results data is sourced from Track Shack Racing's public records and cannot be deleted from our system as it would be re-aggregated from the public source. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only:

- With your explicit consent
- With third-party service providers as described in Section 3
- To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights
- In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- Access your account information
- Correct inaccurate account information
- Request deletion of your account

Please note:

- Race results are publicly available data from Track Shack Racing and cannot be deleted from our system
- Microsoft Clarity analytics cannot be disabled on a per-user basis
- To request corrections to race results, contact Track Shack Racing directly

To exercise your account rights, contact us at gary@falchionconsulting.com

While our App has no explicit age restriction, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

## 8. Race Data Disclaimer

Villains Vault is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by The Walt Disney Company or runDisney. Race results are sourced from publicly available data provided by Track Shack Racing, the official timing provider for runDisney events.

We aggregate this public data from Track Shack's systems via backend scraping. Track Shack Racing does not track or collect any information about users of Villains Vault. For corrections to race results, contact Track Shack Racing directly.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
